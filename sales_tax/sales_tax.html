Sales Tax Calculator Description

This Sales Tax Calculator helps you determine the amount of sales tax you need to pay on a given purchase.
Simply enter the before-tax price of the item and the sales tax rate to calculate the total sales tax and the final price after tax.
Follow these steps:
1. Enter the price of the item before tax in the "Before Tax Price" field.
2. Enter the applicable sales tax rate in the "Sales Tax Rate (%)" field.
3. Click on the "Calculate Sales Tax" button to see the results.
The calculator will display:
- The price of the item before tax.
- The calculated sales tax amount.
- The total price after including the sales tax.
