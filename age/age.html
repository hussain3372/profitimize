Whether you're planning a birthday, organizing events, or simply curious about your exact age, this calculator provides an accurate and immediate result.

To use the Age Calculator, follow these steps:

1. **Enter Date of Birth:** Begin by entering your date of birth in the designated input field. This is crucial as it serves as the starting point for the age calculation.

2. **Enter Current Date:** Next, input the current date in the second date field. This allows the calculator to determine the total time elapsed since your birth.

3. **View Current Time:** The calculator also displays the current time, adding a dynamic element to your age calculation. This feature updates in real-time, ensuring your results are as precise as possible.

4. **Calculate Age:** Once both dates are entered, simply click the "Calculate Age" button. The calculator will process the information and display your age in years, months, and days. This comprehensive breakdown provides a clear understanding of your age.
