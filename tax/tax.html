Tax Calculator

Results:

Description

The Tax Calculator helps you estimate your taxes based on your salary and the selected tax year.
Simply enter your salary amount and choose the relevant tax year from the dropdown menu.
Click on the "Calculate Tax" button to see the results.
The calculator will display the following results:

- Monthly Salary: The portion of your salary earned each month before tax.
- Monthly Tax: The amount of tax you need to pay each month.
- Salary After Tax: Your monthly salary after tax deductions.
- Yearly Salary Without Tax: Your annual salary before tax.
- Yearly Salary With Tax: Your annual salary after tax deductions.
- Yearly Tax: The total amount of tax you need to pay for the year.
