Time Calculator

The Time Calculator is a simple and efficient tool designed to help you calculate the time required to travel a certain distance at a given speed. This calculator is particularly useful for planning trips and understanding travel durations based on different speeds and distances.


Key Features:

User-Friendly Interface: The calculator features an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, making it accessible for users of all ages and technical backgrounds.


Input Fields: Users can input the speed in kilometers per hour (km/h) and the distance in kilometers (km) they plan to travel.


Calculate Button: Once the speed and distance are entered, users can simply click the "Calculate Time" button to get the result.
